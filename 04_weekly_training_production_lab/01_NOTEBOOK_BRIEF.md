# NOTEBOOK BRIEF

## Notebook Name
Weekly Training Production Lab

## Purpose
Create a source grounded production notebook that helps Jeremy turn training videos, transcripts, guides, notes, and knowledge documents into a repeatable weekly training system for Loan Factory loan officers.

## Audience
Loan Factory loan officers, team leaders, and the future internal training and portal system.

## Questions This Notebook Should Answer
1. What is the best weekly email structure
2. What makes each issue short, tactical, useful, and easy to consume
3. How should transcripts, guides, and notes be converted into team and company email variants
4. How should each issue be repurposed into portal, video, social, and markdown assets
5. What parts should stay manual versus become automated later

## What Belongs In This Notebook
1. Weekly training email structure
2. Team version and company version rules
3. Transcript to email workflows
4. Guide to email workflows
5. Email repurposing workflows
6. Knowledge archive rules
7. Future workflow automation hooks

## What Does Not Belong In This Notebook
1. Deep tool research unless it directly supports training production
2. Random company documents that do not help training output
3. General marketing theory with no repurposing value

## Priority Source Types
1. Training guides
2. NotebookLM transcripts or summaries
3. Example emails
4. Video scripts
5. Content repurposing frameworks
6. Internal style guides
7. Team feedback and performance notes

## Output Types We Want
1. Weekly issue templates
2. Variant rules
3. Production workflows
4. Repurposing workflows
5. Manual versus automation boundaries
6. Asset checklists
7. Markdown archive recommendations

## Update Cadence
Weekly while the knowledge base is being built, then monthly review plus as needed updates when tools, workflows, or training priorities change.
