# NOTEBOOK BRIEF

## Notebook Name
Google AI for Loan Officers

## Purpose
Create a source grounded knowledge system that explains how Loan Factory loan officers can use Google AI tools in practical mortgage business workflows.

## Audience
Loan Factory loan officers, team leaders, and the future internal training and portal system.

## Questions This Notebook Should Answer
1. Which Google AI tools matter most for loan officers right now
2. What each tool is best at in plain English
3. How each tool maps to borrower communication, realtor outreach, content creation, research, workflow support, and training production
4. Which use cases are beginner friendly versus more advanced
5. Which repeated use cases should become markdown documents, Gems, templates, checklists, or training lessons

## What Belongs In This Notebook
1. Gemini app
2. Gemini Gems
3. NotebookLM
4. Google AI Studio where relevant to experimentation and workflow design
5. Gemini in Docs, Sheets, Slides, Drive, Gmail, Meet, Vids, and other Workspace surfaces if available to the team
6. Practical workflow examples for loan officers

## What Does Not Belong In This Notebook
1. Loan Factory internal process rules unless directly needed for context
2. TerraPlus specific operational procedures
3. Generic sales training that is not tied to Google AI usage
4. Deep technical developer documentation that does not help loan officers in the field

## Priority Source Types
1. Official Google Help and product documentation
2. Google Workspace product pages
3. Google Labs and AI feature explainers
4. Internal markdown files created for this project
5. Examples of real loan officer use cases and training notes
6. Video transcripts or webinars about Google AI workflows

## Output Types We Want
1. Tool maps
2. Use case maps
3. Beginner workflows
4. Advanced workflows
5. Prompt ideas
6. Training lesson candidates
7. Markdown document recommendations
8. Gem recommendations
9. Gap reports

## Update Cadence
Weekly while the knowledge base is being built, then monthly review plus as needed updates when tools, workflows, or training priorities change.
