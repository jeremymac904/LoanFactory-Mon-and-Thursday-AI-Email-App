## Workflow

1. Pull the top active threads first
2. Bucket them into borrower, realtor, and internal ops work
3. Ask AI for prioritization or first-pass wording only
4. Review anything sensitive before it leaves draft mode
5. Move confirmed next steps into the CRM or follow-up queue

## Useful Prompt Angle

`Sort these threads into borrower, realtor, and ops buckets. Call out urgent items, suggest one first-pass reply draft per bucket, and flag anything that needs human review.`
