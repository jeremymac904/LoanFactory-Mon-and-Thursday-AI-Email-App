PASS 2 working internal format for topic-bank support.

## Ticket Shape

1. File or scenario identifier
2. Current blocker in one sentence
3. What has already been checked
4. What decision or action is needed next
5. Why timing matters

## Quality Rules

1. One blocker only
2. No wall of text
3. No assumptions disguised as facts
4. State the requested next move clearly
