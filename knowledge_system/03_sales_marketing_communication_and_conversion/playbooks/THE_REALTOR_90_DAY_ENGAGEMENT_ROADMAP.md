PASS 2 working roadmap for internal training and topic development.

## Month 1: Visibility

1. Show up consistently
2. Share one useful market or workflow note
3. Avoid the generic `checking in` opener

## Month 2: Value

1. Bring one co-marketing or content idea
2. Offer one workflow improvement or tech tip
3. Show how you reduce friction, not just how you close loans

## Month 3: Ask

1. Make the ask after value has already been shown
2. Keep it simple and direct
3. Tie the ask to how future deals will move cleaner

## What To Avoid

1. Asking for business before bringing value
2. Generic lender or LO pitch language
3. Weak follow-up after a promising conversation
