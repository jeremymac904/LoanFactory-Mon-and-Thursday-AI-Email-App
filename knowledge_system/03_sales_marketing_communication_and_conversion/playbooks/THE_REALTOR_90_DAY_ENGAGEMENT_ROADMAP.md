# THE_REALTOR_90_DAY_ENGAGEMENT_ROADMAP

## Purpose

Provide a first pass 90 day relationship rhythm for building consistent realtor engagement without relying on generic check in language.

## Roadmap

### Days 1 To 30

1. Introduce a clear value proposition
2. Share one useful market or process insight only if verified
3. Ask one low friction question about their current business focus
4. Log what matters to that relationship

### Days 31 To 60

1. Follow up with a relevant resource, checklist, or communication asset
2. Share a short example of how you help clients move smoothly through the process
3. Keep the ask light and relationship focused
4. Avoid repeating the same opening line from month one

### Days 61 To 90

1. Offer a practical collaboration idea if appropriate
2. Share one concise training or content asset that helps their audience
3. Ask whether the current rhythm is useful or should change
4. Decide whether the relationship should move into a deeper cadence, a lighter cadence, or pause

## Engagement Rules

1. Every touch should have a reason
2. Do not send generic checking in messages
3. Keep messages brief and relevant
4. Track what the realtor actually responds to
5. Respect relationship fatigue signals

## Review Gate

Before this becomes a live outreach sequence, confirm:

1. The value statements are real and supportable
2. The cadence fits the current channel strategy
3. Compliance sensitive language has been reviewed
