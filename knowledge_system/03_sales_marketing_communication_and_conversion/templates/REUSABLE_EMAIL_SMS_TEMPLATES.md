## New Lead Reply

```text
[Name], saw your note and wanted to make this easy.

The next best step is [one clear step].
If helpful, send me [one item] and I will tighten the next move from there.
```

## Borrower Update

```text
Here is what is confirmed right now:
[confirmed item]

Here is what is still pending:
[pending item]

Next step:
[one next action]
```

## Refi Or Re-Engagement Touch

```text
Quick check on your side: has anything changed with the property, timing, or goals since we last talked?

If it makes sense, I can map the next step from there.
```

## Realtor Touch

```text
Thought of you because this is one workflow or content idea you could use this week:
[one idea]

If you want it, I can send the cleaner version over.
```

## Review Request

```text
Appreciate working with you on this file.

If the experience felt strong, a short review helps more than you probably think.
```

## Tone Rules

1. Plain language beats jargon
2. One next step beats three asks
3. Remove every line that sounds automated
4. Keep promises and certainty aligned to what is actually known
