## Borrower Document Request

```text
Subject: Quick next step for your file

Hi [Name],

To keep things moving, I need [document or item] from you.

Why this matters:
[Short reason in plain English.]

Please send it by [verified date or time frame].
If anything is unclear, reply here and I will help.

[Your name]
```

## Borrower Status Update

```text
Hi [Name], quick update:

Current status: [confirmed status]
Next step: [confirmed next step]
What I need from you, if anything: [single ask]

I will update you again when [verified trigger].
```

## New Lead First Touch

```text
Hi [Name], this is [Your name].

I saw your inquiry about [context].
Happy to help.

What would be most useful right now:
[option one] or [option two]?
```

## Lead Reengagement

```text
Hi [Name], circling back in case this is still on your radar.

If timing changed, no problem.
If it would help, I can send a short overview of the next steps based on where you are now.
```

## Realtor Relationship Touch

```text
Hi [Name],

I wanted to send over one useful item for your clients this week:
[resource or short insight].

If helpful, I can also put together a version you can share directly.
```

## SMS Rules

1. One message, one purpose
2. Use plain English
3. Avoid dense explanations
4. Move complex items to email or call
5. Do not use unverified urgency

## Email Rules

1. Keep the subject line specific
2. Put the ask near the top
3. Use spacing to make the action obvious
4. Remove filler before send
