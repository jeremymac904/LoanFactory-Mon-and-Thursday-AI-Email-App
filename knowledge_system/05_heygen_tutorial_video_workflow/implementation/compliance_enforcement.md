# Compliance Enforcement

The app should enforce caution, not pretend certainty.

## V1 Enforcement Rules

1. Keep review-sensitive language visible in the script
2. Do not auto-remove caution notes before render
3. Keep final attach behind a human video review gate
4. Use a placeholder footer in mock mode instead of claiming a final approved legal line

## Draft Categories That Need Extra Review

1. Rates
2. Fees
3. Product eligibility
4. Approvals
5. Borrower-specific guidance
6. Policy or operational claims that sound settled

## Mock App Rule

1. Mock script generation may append a placeholder compliance footer
2. Placeholder text is not approved policy and should say so directly

## Needs Factual Validation

1. Exact required compliance footer text, if any, for this internal video workflow

## Needs Compliance Review

1. Any final disclosure text
2. Any live tutorial video that uses real mortgage advice, borrower scenarios, or policy guidance
