# EMAIL TO PORTAL CARD WORKFLOW

## Purpose

Define how a weekly training issue should be compressed into a portal ready knowledge card.

## Source Basis

1. `04_weekly_training_production_lab/01_NOTEBOOK_BRIEF.md`
2. `04_weekly_training_production_lab/04_DOMAIN_MAP.md`
3. `04_weekly_training_production_lab/08_NOTEBOOKLM_REFINEMENT_PROMPTS.md`
4. `04_weekly_training_production_lab/09_MARKDOWN_BUILD_QUEUE.md`

## Current Status

First pass workflow for future portal compatibility.

## Needs Factual Validation

1. Final portal field requirements
2. Final portal character limits and tagging rules
3. Whether cards need owner, approval, or expiration metadata

## Needs Compliance Review

1. Any card that restates product guidance, borrower advice, or sales language as a portable snippet

## Workflow

1. Start from the final approved weekly email
2. Extract the single takeaway the portal card should teach
3. Rewrite the lesson into a short title, summary, and action list
4. Remove email specific framing and greetings
5. Add tags for lane, audience, and content type
6. Add source reference back to the weekly issue
7. Flag any card that needs factual or compliance review before publishing

## Portal Card Shape

1. Title
2. One sentence summary
3. Three short action bullets
4. Optional linked resource
5. Source reference

## Quality Checks

1. The card stands alone without the full email
2. The main action is obvious
3. No sensitive claims are carried over without review
