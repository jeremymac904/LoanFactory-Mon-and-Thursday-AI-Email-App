# AI Training Automation

Automated system that generates and delivers AI-powered sales training content to loan officer teams on a recurring schedule. This system supports the company-wide bi-weekly AI training program delivered to 60–150 Loan Factory originators per session.

## Problem

Consistent team training requires hours of manual content creation every week. Leaders either skip training or recycle stale material because there's no time to build fresh, market-relevant content at scale.

## System

An automated pipeline that generates targeted training emails using AI, customized to current market conditions and team focus areas, and delivers them on a set schedule.

## How It Works

1. System pulls current market data and training topics
2. AI generates training content tailored to the team's focus areas
3. Formatted emails are assembled and scheduled
4. Delivered automatically on cadence (2x per week)
5. Content stays current without manual intervention

## Stack

TypeScript, Vercel

## Outcome

Eliminated manual training content creation while ensuring loan officers receive consistent, market-relevant education. Now supports company-wide AI adoption training for Loan Factory's origination teams.
