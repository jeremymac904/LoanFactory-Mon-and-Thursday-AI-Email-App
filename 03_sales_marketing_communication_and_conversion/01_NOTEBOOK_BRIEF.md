# NOTEBOOK BRIEF

## Notebook Name
Sales, Marketing, Communication, and Conversion

## Purpose
Create a source grounded communication and growth notebook that helps Loan Factory loan officers market better, communicate better, build authority, and convert more leads using practical AI assisted methods.

## Audience
Loan Factory loan officers, team leaders, and the future internal training and portal system.

## Questions This Notebook Should Answer
1. Which communication and marketing patterns are strongest in the sources
2. What works best for borrowers, realtors, leads, and personal brand content
3. What sounds human and confident versus robotic or generic
4. Which patterns build trust, authority, replies, and conversion
5. Which repeated assets should become templates, markdown documents, Gems, or weekly training lessons

## What Belongs In This Notebook
1. Borrower communication
2. Realtor outreach
3. Lead follow up
4. Social content
5. Short video content
6. Email communication
7. Objection handling
8. Personal branding for loan officers

## What Does Not Belong In This Notebook
1. Deep operational workflow manuals unless directly tied to messaging
2. Tool documentation that does not improve sales or communication
3. Random marketing theory with no practical application

## Priority Source Types
1. Sales training references
2. Marketing frameworks
3. Communication examples
4. Borrower education examples
5. Realtor outreach examples
6. High performing content examples
7. Internal style documents and team messaging guides

## Output Types We Want
1. Messaging pattern maps
2. Audience use case maps
3. Template recommendations
4. Content system recommendations
5. Weekly training lesson candidates
6. Gem recommendations
7. Gap analysis

## Update Cadence
Weekly while the knowledge base is being built, then monthly review plus as needed updates when tools, workflows, or training priorities change.
