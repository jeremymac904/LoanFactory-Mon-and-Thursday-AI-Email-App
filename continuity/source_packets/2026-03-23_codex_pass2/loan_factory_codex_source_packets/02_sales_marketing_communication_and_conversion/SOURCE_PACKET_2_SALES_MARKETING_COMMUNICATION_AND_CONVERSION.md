## Audience Framework

### Borrowers
**Main goals:** Reduce anxiety, explain clearly, build trust, keep the borrower engaged.

**Big mistakes:** Mortgage jargon, too much talking, weak listening, focusing only on rate.

**High trust communication:** Plain language, diagnostic questions, math that helps the borrower see the bigger picture.

**Weak communication:** Legalese, robotic status updates, defensive responses to shopping.

**Best AI assisted use cases:** Plain language rewrites, first pass borrower texts, checklist based follow up.

**Best training lessons:** Active listening, math of savings, advisor tone.

### Realtors
**Main goals:** Become a value first strategic partner.

**Big mistakes:** Asking for business before bringing value, generic LO pitches, weak follow up.

**High trust communication:** Give before asking, local value, co marketing, useful lead support.

**Best AI assisted use cases:** Co branded content ideas, market briefings, outreach drafts.

**Best training lessons:** 90 day engagement strategy, co marketing, value first messaging.

### Leads and Prospects
**Main goals:** Fast response, authority, personalization, follow up consistency.

**Big mistakes:** Slow response, quitting after one or two attempts, generic automation.

**High trust communication:** Fast, personal, relevant, clear, and useful.

**Best AI assisted use cases:** Lead reply drafts, follow up scripts, video or text hooks.

**Best training lessons:** 5 minute response, persistent cadence, anti robotic follow up.

### Public Content Audiences
**Main goals:** Build authority, educate, debunk myths, generate inbound.

**Big mistakes:** Salesy graphics, generic corporate content, weak short form video use.

**Best AI assisted use cases:** Caption drafts, script ideas, local content angles, review repurposing.

**Best training lessons:** Short form video engine, social proof, local authority content.
