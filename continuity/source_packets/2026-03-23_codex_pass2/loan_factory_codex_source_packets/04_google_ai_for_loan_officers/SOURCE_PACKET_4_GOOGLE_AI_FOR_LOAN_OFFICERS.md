# Source Packet 4
# Google AI for Loan Officers

## Packet Purpose

This packet defines how the Google AI lane should support the twice weekly internal AI tips email system for Loan Factory loan officers.

It should directly support:
1. Monday and Thursday training emails
2. short video demo ideas
3. prompt assets and Gems
4. quick start guides
5. safe usage boundaries

## Practical Operating Map

### Most useful Google AI tools
1. Gemini Advanced
2. NotebookLM
3. Gemini Gems
4. Google Opal or Workspace Studio
5. Document AI
6. Gemini in Workspace, especially Gmail, Docs, and Sheets
7. Google Vids and Veo class video tools

### Plain English uses
**Gemini Advanced:** reasoning, drafting, image or photo analysis, structured help  
**NotebookLM:** source grounded search and synthesis with citations  
**Gemini Gems:** repeatable mini assistants for common tasks  
**Opal:** simple workflow or mini app builder  
**Document AI:** borrower document extraction  
**Gemini in Workspace:** fast help in the apps LOs already use  
**Video tools:** internal demos, listing or training visuals

## Asset Build Plan

### Quick start guides

#### GEMINI_WORKSPACE_QUICKSTART.md
**Purpose:** Teach beginner LOs how to use Gemini in Gmail and Docs.  
**Sections:** Workspace side panel, morning briefing, draft reply, tone refinement.  
**Needs compliance review:** Do not generate loan terms without human review.

#### NOTEBOOKLM_BASICS_FOR_LOS.md
**Purpose:** Teach LOs how to build a digital brain for guideline or document research.  
**Sections:** What it is, how to structure a notebook, configure chat, use cited answers, audio overview.  
**Needs factual validation:** Confirm actual notebook limits and tier access.

### Workflow docs

#### INBOX_TRIAGE_WORKFLOW.md
**Purpose:** Morning workflow for sorting and summarizing borrower emails.  
**Sections:** problem, prompt, execution steps, urgent flag handling.

#### CRM_NOTES_AUTOMATION_WORKFLOW.md
**Purpose:** Turn borrower call audio into CRM ready notes.  
**Sections:** recording, upload, formatting prompt, MOSO mapping.  
**Needs compliance review:** Call recording consent rules.

### Prompt and Gem support docs

#### LO_CORE_PROMPT_LIBRARY.md
**Purpose:** Daily copy paste prompt bank for common tasks.  
**Sections:** email drafting, objections, realtor outreach, social content.  
**Needs compliance review:** Marketing and communication prompt guardrails.

#### GEMINI_GEMS_BUILD_GUIDE.md
**Purpose:** Explain how to build repeatable Gems for mortgage work.  
**Sections:** what a Gem is, example Gem specs, sharing notes, best practices.  
**Needs factual validation:** Gem sharing limits in the company environment.

#### HAPPA_SALES_ENGINE_PROMPTS.md
**Purpose:** Prompt library for Hook, Align, Personalize, Pitch, Action.  
**Sections:** framework, ghosted lead, rate objection, browsing lead, follow up.  
**Needs compliance review:** Sales language and UDAAP risk.

### Email topic banks

#### MONDAY_TRAINING_EMAIL_BANK.md
**Purpose:** Quick win Monday topics focused on time saving and AI adoption.  
**Sections:** quarterly banks, quick win template, Monday topic queue.

#### THURSDAY_TRAINING_EMAIL_BANK.md
**Purpose:** Thursday topics focused on sales, objections, conversion, and value creation.  
**Sections:** outreach, conversion, branding, strategy topics.

### Video support

#### VIDEO_DEMO_SCRIPTS_AND_ASSETS.md
**Purpose:** 60 second internal video scripts for the highest value AI workflows.  
**Sections:** inbox triage, audio to CRM, NotebookLM setup, first Gem, visual content demo.  
**Needs factual validation:** Tool interface steps if they change.

### Safety and support docs

#### AI_GOVERNANCE_AND_NPI_SECURITY.md
**Purpose:** Hard rules on what data can and cannot be pasted into AI tools.  
**Sections:** redaction rules, safe versus unsafe tool use, NPI rules, review boundaries.  
**Needs factual validation:** Company specific enterprise terms and storage rules.  
**Needs compliance review:** Must be approved before treated as policy.

#### COMPLIANCE_DISCLAIMER_SNIPPETS.md
**Purpose:** Copy paste disclaimers and safety language for scaled communication.  
**Sections:** email footer language, SMS opt out, rate quote disclaimer, Equal Housing language.  
**Needs compliance review:** Mandatory.
