# NOTEBOOK BRIEF

## Notebook Name
Loan Factory and TerraPlus Operations

## Purpose
Create a source grounded operations notebook that captures how Loan Factory loan officers and team leaders can work smarter inside company systems, portal tools, and supported AI assisted workflows.

## Audience
Loan Factory loan officers, team leaders, and the future internal training and portal system.

## Questions This Notebook Should Answer
1. What are the main operational systems and workflows that show up in the sources
2. How does the real day to day loan officer workflow work in plain English
3. Where are the biggest bottlenecks, delays, or communication breakdowns
4. Where can AI safely help with drafting, summaries, training, checklists, or workflow support
5. What should remain human controlled versus standardized or automated later

## What Belongs In This Notebook
1. Loan Factory internal process references
2. TerraPlus portal or tool references
3. Role based workflow patterns
4. Operational templates and checklists
5. Communication standards that support workflow efficiency
6. Training opportunities tied to real operations

## What Does Not Belong In This Notebook
1. Broad Google AI capability unless tied to real internal use
2. Generic motivational sales content
3. External public marketing strategies that do not connect to daily operations

## Priority Source Types
1. Internal process documents
2. Portal screenshots or guides
3. Training notes
4. Team workflow documents
5. Real examples of operational friction or handoff issues
6. Company approved communication examples where available

## Output Types We Want
1. Workflow maps
2. Role maps
3. Bottleneck analysis
4. AI assist opportunities
5. Manual versus automated boundaries
6. Template recommendations
7. Gem recommendations
8. Markdown build recommendations

## Update Cadence
Weekly while the knowledge base is being built, then monthly review plus as needed updates when tools, workflows, or training priorities change.
