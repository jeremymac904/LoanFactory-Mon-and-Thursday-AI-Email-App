# RESEARCH MISSION

## Mission

Use this notebook to produce practical, source grounded intelligence that helps build a better internal training system and future product for Loan Factory loan officers.

## Core Requirements

1. Stay tightly scoped to this notebook domain.
2. Favor practical loan officer use cases over theory.
3. Make the output easy to turn into markdown knowledge files.
4. Flag what should become a reusable Gem.
5. Flag what should become a weekly training lesson.
6. Distinguish beginner friendly guidance from more advanced workflows.
7. Surface gaps honestly instead of guessing.

## What Good Output Looks Like

1. Clear and practical
2. Easy to understand
3. Specific to mortgage and loan officer work
4. Structured enough to copy into docs
5. Useful for email training, portal content, and knowledge base building

## What Bad Output Looks Like

1. Generic AI advice
2. Repeated marketing fluff
3. Tool hype with no workflow value
4. Long summaries with no decisions
5. Mixed domains that belong in other notebooks
